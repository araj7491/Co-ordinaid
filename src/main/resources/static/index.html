Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is the Co-ordinaid?

A centralized platform to manage volunteers, assignments, skills, timesheets, and nonprofits for community service and engagement.

Q2: How do I add a new volunteer?

Click on the "Volunteer" tab and use the add button to input volunteer details such as name, contact, and skills.

Q3: Can I track volunteer hours?

Yes, use the "Timesheet" tab to log and view volunteer working hours against assignments.

Q4: What are assignments?

Assignments represent tasks or roles assigned to volunteers by nonprofits. You can manage them under the "Assignment" tab.

Q5: Who can use this system?

This system is ideal for nonprofits, volunteer coordinators, and anyone managing community-driven events or social service initiatives.
